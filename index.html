Project Overview

Happy Worms

Happy Worms is a small local business owned by Cassandra Neri that sells composting worms along with related products. Our goal is to promote the healthy hobbies of vermicomposting and gardening using the perfect worm composting starter kit. Worm composting is the proverbial win-win situation that gives you a convenient way to dispose of unwanted food waste and provides you happy worms which then produce the best organic fertilizer–or as gardeners say, “BLACK GOLD” for your garden.
